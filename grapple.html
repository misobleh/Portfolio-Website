Sticky Hand Grapple



What?

A device designed to shoot out a retractable sticky hand. The sticky hand has the ability to latch and grab onto small objects. The entire projectile can then be retracted using a hand crank. My role was to design and make the hand crank, chassis, and assist with fabricating other parts.




How? (Mechanism)

The heart of the shooter is responsible for the winding and firing of the sticky hand. While the projectile is being retracted, a spring from the plunger gets compressed. When the user pulls the trigger, a rubber band unlocks the gears which allows the spring to fire the sticky hand.




How? (Hand Crank)

To construct the hand crank, a 3D model along with an engineering drawing was made. This was to ensure that the dimensions were correct. The crank features threaded holes for set screws, allowing the parts to lock together. The parts were machined using a lathe, a mill, and a bandsaw.




Results

A prototype was successfully made. The device fufilled its intended purpose and the winding mechanism was smooth. however, the shooting distance could be improved by using a stronger spring and metal gears. Plastic gears were used which degraded quickly causing slipping.
